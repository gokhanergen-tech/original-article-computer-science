## 2. Differential Evolution (DE)

- **Year:** 1997
- **Authors:** Storn & Price
- **Original Paper:** [Link PDF](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1023/A:1008202821328)

**Summary:**  
Differential Evolution (DE) is a **population-based evolutionary algorithm**:

- Candidate solutions are combined using **vector differences between randomly chosen individuals**.
- Applies **mutation, crossover, and selection** to evolve the population.
- Highly effective for **continuous, nonlinear, high-dimensional optimization problems**.
